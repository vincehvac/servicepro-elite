## 📱 **HOW TO INSTALL MOBILE APP**

### **iPhone/iPad (iOS):**
1. Open **Safari**
2. Go to: https://vincehvac.github.io/servicepro-elite/mobile-tech.html
3. Tap **Share** button (square with arrow)
4. Tap **"Add to Home Screen"**
5. Tap **"Add"**
6. ✅ App installed!

### **Android:**
1. Open **Chrome**
2. Go to: https://vincehvac.github.io/servicepro-elite/mobile-tech.html
3. Tap **menu** (3 dots)
4. Tap **"Add to Home Screen"**
5. Tap **"Install"**
6. ✅ App installed!
